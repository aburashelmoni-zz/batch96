About Us

We are the students of Shitakunda Govt High School at Shitakunda, Chittagong We passed SSC in 1996 with warmth and pride.

First day at School means the first step towards painting a bright future of a student. It was an amazing experience with a mix of excitement. It was an unforgettable and lifelong memory for us.

From first day we consider each friends as our own friend. This is another day of pride, a token of love – which could not be achieved without those friends.
